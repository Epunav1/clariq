3. The Problem We're Solving

Meet Your Typical E-Commerce Seller

Let's call her Sarah. She runs a $500K/year Shopify store selling home decor. She also sells on Amazon, TikTok Shop, and Etsy. Last year, she started experimenting with Facebook ads.

Sarah's smart. She knows she should be data-driven. But here's what her mornings look like:

  • 6:00am - Check Shopify dashboard (5 min)
  • 6:10am - Check Amazon Seller Central (10 min)
  • 6:25am - Check TikTok analytics (8 min)
  • 6:35am - Check email for overnight orders (5 min)
  • 6:45am - Manually check inventory levels (15 min)
  • 7:00am - Try to piece it all together (10 min)

Total: 53 minutes just to answer "How did we do yesterday?"

And she's doing this alone. No analytics team. No data scientist.

The Core Problems

Problem 1: Data Fragmentation

Her business is spread across 5 platforms, each with their own dashboard. No unified view. When she wants to know "What's my real profit margin across all channels?" she has to manually export CSVs and build a spreadsheet. By the time she's done, the data is stale.

Problem 2: Analysis Paralysis

Even when she has data, what does it mean? She doesn't have SQL skills. She doesn't know if her 5% profit margin is healthy or if she should be alarmed. She makes decisions based on gut feel.

Problem 3: Execution Lag

Once Sarah finally decides to do something—"I should lower prices on slow-moving inventory" or "I should email my inactive customers"—it takes hours to execute. By the time her emails go out, the moment has passed.

Problem 4: Cost Barrier

Serious analytics tools (Power BI, Tableau, Looker) cost $500-5,000+/month. Sarah's budget is $100-300/month for tools. She can't afford proper analytics.

Problem 5: No Actionable Insights

Most dashboards show data. They don't tell Sarah what to do. She needs someone to say: "Your inventory is 40% too high. Lower prices by 15% on these 3 products. You'll clear it in 10 days and recover margin."

The Impact

  • 8-10 hours per week wasted on data collection
  • Missed opportunities (slow inventory doesn't get optimized)
  • Bad decisions (based on incomplete or stale data)
  • Customer acquisition cost too high (no data to optimize)
  • Profit margins eroding (no visibility into profitability by product/channel)

A typical seller leaves $50K-$100K+ on the table every year because they can't connect their data to action.

5. Our Solution: Advisory + Execution

What Clariq Does

Clariq is different because we're not building "yet another analytics dashboard." We're building a business operations engine.

Three Layers

Layer 1: Unified Data

Connect all your stores (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, etc.) in one place. We handle the API complexity. You get one source of truth.

Layer 2: Smart Recommendations (Advisory)

We use AI to analyze your data in real-time and recommend actions:

  • "Your inventory on Widget X is 40% too high. Margin will be negative in 2 weeks."
  • "5 customers haven't purchased in 90 days. Engagement email will recover 30% of them."
  • "Your Facebook ROAS dropped 20% this week. Pause these campaigns."

Layer 3: Automated Execution (The Secret Sauce)

Here's where we differ from every other tool: We execute the actions.

  • Price adjustment? Clariq updates prices across Shopify + Amazon automatically.
  • Email campaign? Clariq drafts the email, picks the send time, sends it, tracks results.
  • Promotion? Clariq creates discount codes, schedules emails, monitors performance.
  • Inventory rebalance? Clariq recommends moves and can execute them automatically.

User clicks "Approve" → Clariq executes → Results show up in the dashboard next day.

The Unique Promise

You don't hire an analyst. Clariq is your analyst.

You don't spend 8 hours/week on data. Clariq does that.

You don't manually execute campaigns. Clariq does that too.

You focus on strategy, product, customer relationships. Everything else is automated.

10. Risk Analysis & Mitigation

Market & Competition Risk

Risk: Triple Whale Expands to Multi-Platform

Likelihood: High | Impact: High

Mitigation: Move fast. Hit multi-platform in Year 1-2 before they do. Build community loyalty. Our execution advantage is harder to copy than multi-platform connection.

Risk: E-Commerce Growth Stalls

Likelihood: Medium | Impact: High

Mitigation: TAM is still massive ($10M+ sellers). We're not betting on growth—we're solving an existing pain point. Recession actually helps (sellers cut analyst headcount, buy our platform instead).

Product & Execution Risk

Risk: AI Recommendations Don't Work Well

Likelihood: Low | Impact: Critical

Mitigation: Data scientist has track record with recommendation systems. We're building hybrid system (rules + AI, not pure AI). We'll test with 100 beta users first before scaling.

Risk: API Integrations Break

Likelihood: Medium | Impact: Medium

Mitigation: We have robust error handling for each platform. Rate limiting is built in. We maintain relationships with platform developer relations teams. Fast response time to API changes.

Customer & Revenue Risk

Risk: Churn Higher Than Expected (8% vs 3% target)

Likelihood: Medium | Impact: High

Mitigation: We're obsessed with customer success. Every customer gets onboarding call. Monthly check-ins. 1:1 support. We track NPS and act fast on feedback.

Risk: CAC Higher Than Projected

Likelihood: Medium | Impact: Medium

Mitigation: We're starting with organic/community (low CAC). Paid ads only after organic is working. We have multiple channels (content, partnerships, community). If one channel doesn't work, we pivot.

Funding Risk

Risk: Fundraising Delays Series A

Likelihood: Low | Impact: High

Mitigation: We don't need Series A to survive. We're profitable from Year 1. Series A just accelerates growth. If we can't raise, we bootstrap and grow slowly. Still works.

11. Technology & Security

Tech Stack

We're using battle-tested, proven technologies:

  • Backend: Python + FastAPI (modern, fast, scalable)
  • Database: PostgreSQL (reliable, handles analytics queries well)
  • Frontend: React (most popular, best talent pool)
  • Deployment: Render (scales automatically, handles traffic spikes)
  • AI/ML: Claude API + custom models for recommendations
  • Background jobs: APScheduler for automated tasks

Architecture

We've built the API layer already. MVP adds:

  • Shopify OAuth integration (connect stores securely)
  • Data sync pipeline (pull data every 2 hours)
  • Recommendation engine (AI suggestions)
  • Execution layer (auto-update prices, send emails)
  • Dashboard frontend (React)

Security & Compliance

We take data seriously because customer data IS our competitive advantage.

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 with JWT tokens
  • API security: Rate limiting, IP whitelist options, API key rotation
  • Data isolation: Each customer's data is isolated (no cross-contamination)
  • Compliance roadmap:
    • SOC2 Type II (Year 1, Month 6)
    • GDPR compliance (Year 1, immediate)
    • CCPA compliance (Year 1, immediate)
    • LGPD compliance (Brazil, Year 2)
  • Backups: Daily backups, 30-day retention
  • Disaster recovery: <4 hour RTO, <1 hour RPO
