About Clariq

Built for sellers, by sellers

Our Mission

We believe every seller deserves to understand their numbers, regardless of platform or technical skill. Founded by sellers who were tired of complex dashboards and spreadsheets, Clariq makes data analysis simple and accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Clariq started with a simple frustration: seller dashboards are broken. They're overwhelming, disconnected, and require a PhD in data science to understand.

Our founder, Victor Epuna, was running multiple Shopify stores when he realized a pattern—successful sellers ask better questions. So we built a tool that lets anyone ask their data anything, in plain English.

Why We're Different

We don't believe in complexity. No SQL, no pivot tables, no consulting. Just natural language queries and instant answers. We're obsessed with making data accessible, intuitive, and actionable.

Our team is distributed across 4 continents, working 24/7 to support sellers in 120+ countries. We're committed to security, reliability, and your success.
