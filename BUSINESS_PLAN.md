# CLARIQ BUSINESS PLAN

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Clariq** is an AI-powered business intelligence platform for e-commerce sellers that answers data questions in plain English. Instead of spending hours analyzing spreadsheets, sellers simply ask "How much did I sell last week?" or "Which products are most profitable?" and get instant insights across all their sales channels (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, Alibaba, WooCommerce, Etsy, Jumia, etc.).

**Why we will succeed:**
- **Massive pain point:** 10M+ e-commerce sellers worldwide, 73% say data analysis is too hard
- **AI timing:** Natural language interfaces are hot; users prefer voice/text over dashboards
- **Blue ocean market:** No competitor owns the "multi-platform + AI + affordable" segment
- **Unit economics:** 60%+ gross margin on SaaS, $1,200-3,000 annual customer LTV
- **First-mover advantage:** Triple Whale ignores non-Shopify sellers; we go multi-platform from day 1
- **Realistic path to $1M MRR:** 833 customers at $99/mo average = achievable in 18-24 months

**Founding team:** Solo founder with 5+ years e-commerce experience, technical chops (FastAPI, React, databases), and product sense.

## 2. COMPANY DESCRIPTION

### **Business Name:** 
Clariq (Analytics + Clarity)

### **Mission Statement:**
"To empower every e-commerce seller to make data-driven decisions by making business intelligence as simple as asking a question in plain English."

### **Vision Statement:**
"To become the world's most trusted AI advisor for e-commerce, where every seller—regardless of technical skill or budget—has instant access to actionable business intelligence that drives growth, profitability, and scale."

### **Core Problems We Solve:**

**Problem 1: Data Fragmentation**
- Sellers have stores on 3-5 different platforms (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, Etsy, etc.)
- No single place to see all sales, inventory, customers
- Time spent = 8+ hours/week manually pulling reports

**Problem 2: Analysis Paralysis**
- Even when data is accessible, sellers can't analyze it
- Don't know which metrics matter
- Don't have SQL/data analysis skills
- Business decisions are guesses, not data-driven

**Problem 3: Cost Barrier**
- Existing solutions: Tableau ($500+), Power BI ($400+), or hire analyst ($5K+/month)
- Out of budget for 90% of SMBs
- Free tools are too basic

**Problem 4: Speed**
- Traditional dashboards are static; updates are slow
- Decision-making happens in real-time but data is hours/days old
- By the time you see a trend, it's too late to act

### **Solution: Clariq**
- **Connect all stores** (7+ e-commerce platforms)
- **Ask questions** ("What was my best-selling product yesterday?")
- **Get instant answers** (<3 seconds with AI)
- **Affordable** ($99-249/month vs $500-5K+)
- **No SQL/data skills required** (plain English interface)

### **Legal Structure:**
- Sole proprietorship (can transition to LLC/Corp for investment)
- Operating from USA
- Registered for business taxes

## 4. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

### **Core Product: Clariq Analytics Dashboard**

**What it does:**
- Connects to seller's e-commerce platforms (OAuth integration)
- Pulls real-time sales, inventory, customer, and profit data
- Converts plain English questions → SQL queries → instant answers
- Displays results in human-readable format

**Key Features:**

**1. Multi-Platform Connection**
- Shopify (direct API)
- Amazon (Selling Partner API)
- TikTok Shop (API)
- Alibaba (API)
- WooCommerce (direct)
- Etsy (API)
- Jumia (API)
- Custom integrations (via Zapier/webhook)

**2. AI Question Answering**
- Ask: "How much did I sell yesterday?"
- Ask: "Which products have negative profit margin?"
- Ask: "What's my conversion rate by country?"
- Ask: "Show me top 10 customers by LTV"
- Instant answers with charts/tables

**3. Automated Reports**
- Weekly sales digest
- Profit analysis by product
- Inventory warnings
- Customer acquisition trends
- Competitor benchmarking (optional)

**4. Smart Recommendations**
- "These 5 products are losing money — consider discontinuing"
- "You have $50K excess inventory in winter coats — run a promo"
- "This customer has 4-month gaps between purchases — win them back"
- "Revenue dropped 15% — here's why (3 products underperformed)"

**5. Real-Time Alerts**
- Stock running low
- Big order spike (unusual activity)
- Payment failures
- Customer churn risk
- Negative reviews/feedback

**6. Custom Dashboards**
- Drag-and-drop widgets
- KPI tracking
- Goal setting and monitoring
- Team sharing and permissions
