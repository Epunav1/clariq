About Clariq

Built for sellers, by sellers

Our Mission

Every seller deserves to understand their numbers. Whether you're on Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, or Alibaba—your data should talk to you in plain English, not confuse you with complex dashboards.

Our Story

Clariq was born from frustration. Our founder was running multiple stores across platforms and realized the fundamental problem: seller dashboards are broken. They're overwhelming, disconnected, and require technical expertise.

We built Clariq to fix this. Now sellers in 120+ countries ask our AI millions of questions every day and make better business decisions faster.

Why We're Different

We're obsessed with simplicity. No SQL. No spreadsheets. No consulting. Just natural language and instant answers. We're committed to your success, your security, and your privacy.
